Directives in Angular:
  - A Directive is an instructions to the DOM
  - Manipulate the DOM
  - Change Appearance/Behaviour
  - Add/Remove DOM element

Directives Types:
  - Component Directive: Component Directive is the Angular Component. It is a directive with a template.
  - Attribute Directive: Attribute directive is used to change the appearance or behaviour of a DOM element. eg. ngStyle, ngClass
  - Structural Directive: Structural directive is used to add or remove a DOM element on the webpage. eg. ngIf, ngFor, ngSwitch
